Why choose Nubart SYNC?

Unlock Audio for Public Video Displays — Without Hardware or Apps


Public spaces are full of muted screens — digital signage, ads in shop windows, safety instructions at airports, documentaries in waiting rooms. But sound is often forbidden due to noise regulations or social etiquette.

Nubart SYNC solves this with a clean, user-friendly solution: stream audio directly to smartphones via QR code. Visitors scan, select their language, and listen — all in perfect sync with the video.

No special hardware. No Bluetooth, beacons, or apps. Just the video, a QR code, and our cloud-based synchronization technology.

You can embed the QR code on the screen itself, or place it nearby. It works equally well in both indoor and outdoor environments — even with large screens, mosaics, or projection mappings.

How Nubart Sync Works

From silent screens to synchronized multilingual audio

Nubart Sync works by allowing visitors to scan a QR code with their smartphones. They watch a muted video on-site while Nubart's cloud system streams the synchronized audio in their preferred language directly to their device, with no apps or hardware required.

Video playback systems compatible with Nubart Sync

Compatible video player options:

Any screen or player with internet connection

If your device can open a modern browser and has an Internet connection, it should be compatible with Nubart Sync.

Brightsign

Compatible with Brightsign video players with Internet connection, such as HD224. Compatible with Brightsight Author digital signage software.

Micro-mapping or other AV-solutions

As long as your device has Internet connection, you can integrate our API in your custom playback system. Please contact us.

Audio playback systems compatible with Nubart Sync

Compatible audio player options:

Any smartphone

Nubart Sync soundtracks open in a browser. So any smartphone with a browser and the ability to scan a QR code is compatible. No app to download required!
